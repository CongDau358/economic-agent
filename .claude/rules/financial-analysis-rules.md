# Financial Analysis Rules

## Analysis Layers
- Facts: cited observations from reports/news/social.
- Signals: derived directionality (improving, stable, deteriorating).
- Interpretation: business impact and exposure.
- Outlook: bounded forward view with explicit uncertainty.

## Mandatory Dimensions
- Financial performance (revenue, margin, cash flow, leverage).
- Sentiment and narrative momentum.
- Macro and policy sensitivity.

## Risk and Opportunity Logic
- Every risk/opportunity must include:
  - trigger
  - transmission path
  - expected business effect
  - likely horizon (near/medium term)

## Forecast Discipline
- Forecasts are directional and conditional, not deterministic promises.
- Include assumptions and invalidation conditions.
- Default to neutral stance when signals are mixed.
