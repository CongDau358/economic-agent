# Output Format Rules

## Required Output Sections
1. Executive Summary
2. Evidence Snapshot
3. Financial Signals
4. Trend Outlook
5. Risks
6. Opportunities
7. Confidence and Assumptions
8. Citations

## Formatting Constraints
- Keep facts and interpretation in separate sections.
- Use concise bullet points for risks/opportunities.
- Keep trend statements time-bounded (1-3 months, 3-6 months).

## Citation Policy
- Attach citations for all material claims.
- Use format:
  - `[source_type | company | period | doc_id/chunk_id]`
- If a section has no citations, relabel it as assumptions.

## Confidence Reporting
- Provide score `0.0-1.0` and band `HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW`.
- Explain at least two factors affecting confidence.
