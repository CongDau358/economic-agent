# Intent Classification Rules

## /ask
Use for:
- factual queries
- metric lookup
- document retrieval

Examples:
- "FPT revenue 2024"
- "Techcombank profit"

## /analyze
Use for:
- company analysis
- sector analysis
- financial evaluation

Examples:
- "Analyze EV industry"
- "Analyze banking sector"

## /predict
Use for:
- trend prediction
- short-term outlook
- economic direction

Examples:
- "EV market trend next year"

## /report
Use for:
- structured summaries
- full reports
- comparative analysis
