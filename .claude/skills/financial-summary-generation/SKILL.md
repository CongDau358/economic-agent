# Financial Summary Generation Skill

## When to Use
- Produce executive-ready financial intelligence summaries.
- Standardize final outputs for dashboards, APIs, or analyst briefs.

## Inputs
- analyzed facts/signals/outlook
- risk/opportunity items
- citations and confidence output

## Workflow
1. Draft concise executive summary.
2. Present evidence snapshot with top cited drivers.
3. Summarize signals by category.
4. Present trend outlook by time horizon.
5. List risks and opportunities with impact statements.
6. Attach confidence, assumptions, and citations.

## Output Template
- `executive_summary`
- `evidence_snapshot`
- `financial_signals`
- `trend_outlook`
- `risks`
- `opportunities`
- `confidence`
- `assumptions`
- `citations`

## Guardrails
- Keep narrative consistent with evidence confidence.
- Avoid speculative language without assumptions label.
